#+title: numerical analysis

Numerical analysis is the study of algorithms that use numerical approximation (as opposed to symbolic manipulations) for the problems of [[id:1c6c7a06-fafc-4d3f-9eae-eb0f17a78f9b][mathematical analysis]] (as distinguished from discrete mathematics).
